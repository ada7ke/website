About Me

Hey! I'm Ada and I'm the lighting designer at my high school. I started lighting design in 8th grade and have been super passionate about it ever since. (i practically live in the theater at this point). I've participated in over 15 productions, with my most recent and also favorite being Hadestown.

I also love engineering, and am currently working on a bunch of upgrades to our theater. Completed projects include a low-cost cue light system, with complete instructions and code available for anyone to build their own. I'm also working on building a 3D location tracking module to easily convert back and forth from an Augment3d model to the physical lighting plot, and also undertaking a massive project of building my own DMX RGBW LED fixture with a wiggly mirror.
